Frequently Asked Questions

The typical hours of operation for a toy store are:
Monday to Saturday: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM
Sunday: 12:00 PM – 5:00 PM
Some locations may open Saturday–Wednesday from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and Thursday–Friday until 12:00 AM. Please check the store's website or call for specific holiday schedules and hours[web:17][web:20][web:23].

Toy stores usually offer a variety of services including:
- Gift wrapping and personalized recommendations
- Online shopping with home delivery
- In-store play areas for children
- Loyalty programs and exclusive member discounts
- Assistance for age-appropriate toy selection[web:18][web:25][web:26].

You can contact most toy stores by:
- Phone: For example, 02-37612377, Whatsapp: 01270407658
- Email: info@toptoyseg.com
- Visiting the store at addresses such as 40 Mosadak St., Dokki, Giza
- Online contact forms on the store's website[web:19][web:25][web:21].

Most toy stores have a return policy that allows customers to return or exchange items within a specified period, usually 30 days, with a valid receipt. Some stores may offer extended return periods during the holiday season. It's important to check the specific store's return policy for details on conditions, such as the requirement for original packaging and tags[web:22][web:24][web:27].
